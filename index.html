How To Play

How To Play

Hit the mapped key when a falling note touches the line. Timing tiers are Perfect, Good, and Ok.

Lefty mode uses your requested layout: D hi-hat, G/H snare, E/R low tom, T/Y high tom, 3/4 ride, 5/6 crash, and Space for bass.

Use Jam Mode to freestyle with no song, and Start Recording to capture your hits and add them as a playable chart.
